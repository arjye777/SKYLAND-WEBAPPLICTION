About Skyland

A peaceful stay, a memorable meal — under one sky.

SKYLAND Hotel & Restaurant is more than a place to stay. It's where families gather for Sunday lunch, where travelers rest after a long day on the road, and where local flavors meet warm, friendly service.

We've been welcoming guests for over a decade with the same commitment: clean rooms, thoughtful amenities, and a kitchen that feels like home.
